Q: How to prevent powdery mildew on wheat?

Dr. Ramesh Kumar (Agricultural Scientist): Apply sulfur dust or wettable sulfur 25 EC at 2g/liter spray in early morning or late evening. Maintain proper field hygiene and ensure good air circulation.

Q: When is the best time to apply DAP fertilizer?

Priya Singh (Soil Expert): Apply DAP 2-3 weeks after sowing or at the first irrigation. For wheat, optimal time is when 3-4 leaves emerge. Split application is recommended for better results.
